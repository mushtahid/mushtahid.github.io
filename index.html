A bit about CDAS!

The CDAS is an ESRC funded Doctoral Training Centre across 4 universities:

  • University of Leeds (UoL)
  • University of Liverpool (ULIV)
  • University of Manchester (UoM)
  • University of Sheffield (TUOS)
Thus, although my home university is UoM, I get to enjoy being a student of three other UK universities! Learn more about CDAS!
